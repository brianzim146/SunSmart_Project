Welcome to SunSmart!

SunSmart allows you to monitor the amount of UV exposure you will be exposed to. Simply buy a GPS and UV sensor, add the device to your account, and start tracking all those UV rays! Your personal UV exposure history will be recorded and displayed on this lovely web page. Account oweners will recieve real time alerts, exposure history, and even recieve health advice. Be sure to keep track of the type of sunscreen you use, we can recalculate your UV exposure based on the sunscreen you use!

Login to your account, create a new account, or check out the example account!
